REQUIRED LOGO FILES
===================

Please add the following PNG files to this assets/ folder:

1. barcelona.png
   - Barcelona FC logo
   - Transparent background (PNG)
   - Recommended size: 512x512px or larger
   - High quality for AR display

2. realmadrid.png
   - Real Madrid FC logo
   - Transparent background (PNG)
   - Recommended size: 512x512px or larger
   - High quality for AR display

These logos will be displayed in AR with 3D depth effects.
The app will automatically load them from assets/barcelona.png and assets/realmadrid.png
